About Me

Oliver Acquah is a data-driven and detail-oriented Data Analyst with a strong foundation in data analytics, telecommunications engineering, and business intelligence. He holds a Bachelor's degree in Telecommunication Engineering from KNUST, and has been certified in Advanced Data Science and Analytics through a program by Thrive Africa, awarded by Koforidua Technical University. He also holds a certification in Data Analytics Essentials from Cisco.

He specializes in identifying trends that guide strategic decision-making using advanced tools to deliver impactful visualizations, dashboards, and reports. Oliver is committed to helping organizations drive innovation and growth through data.

He has worked on real-world projects across fintech, aviation safety, agriculture, and social media analytics. Highlights include dashboards for PaySwift, GhanaPay, and Cornerstone, plus calibration work for Ghana Civil Aviation Authority.

His toolkit includes Power BI, Excel, SQL, Python and Google Sheets. He excels in cleaning, analyzing, and visualizing data in real business contexts.
